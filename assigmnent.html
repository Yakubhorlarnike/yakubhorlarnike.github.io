Cup-cake

A Cup-cake is a small cake baked in a cup-shaped foil or paper container and typically iced. Ingredients for making cup-cake include
  1. Softened butter
  2. Gold caster sugar
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. Vanilla extract
  5. Self-rasising flour

Pound Cake

Pound cake is a type of cake traditionally made with a pound of four ingrediennts.The ingredients for baking pound cake are;
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
